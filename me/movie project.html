David's best recently watched movies

My top 3 watched movies from the last year


Gravity

This is a movie that mainly focuses on two astronauts working in orbit around the earth that get hit by space debris and find themselves struggling to stay alive. The aerial views from above the earth in space are simply breathtaking.

Avatar 2

This is another masterpiece of a movie by James Cameron. The captivating visuals and storyline of this movie keep you glued to the screen for the 3 hour duration of the movie and leave you wanting more when it ends.

Pulp Fiction

This is a classic Quentin Tarantino masterpiece that stood the test of time. Brilliant Performances from Samuel L. Jackson, Uma Thurman, Bruce Willis and Ving Rhames coupled with the unique camera angles and music easily set it apart as one of the best movies ever made.
